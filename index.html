About me¶

Welcome! This is the personal webpage of Dr Georgios Deskos

Introduction¶

Hi, my name is Georgios Deskos and I am a Senior Research Engineer at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL). I hold a PhD in Computational Fluid Dynamics from Imperial College London, and an MSc and MEng in Civil Engineering from Virginia Tech, USA, and the National Technical University of Athens, Greece, respectively. Currently, my research interests include offshore wind energy, air-sea interactions, as well as catostrophe modelling for offshore wind energy and other energy systems. A short version of my CV can be found here here. whereas a complete list of my publications, ongoing research projects and other activities can be found in the longer version here.
